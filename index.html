High Current Path Uvic Formula SAE

The high current path (HCP) is designed to safely transmit power from the existing battery segments to the rest of the car. The engineering problem is designing the physical layout of the HCP, and any required bus bar(s) or trays, that is safe, within budgetm high performing, and easy to manufacture within the timeline. Also, the HCP must be seamlessly integrated into the existing accumulator housing, battery segments, and connectors, and abide by FSAE rules. The solution involves a press-fit tab, glued, FR4 tray with all components secured with nylon plastic fasteners, insulated with Nomex Paper (not pictured in CAD), and copper bus bars for high current connections. I learned how to design bus bars how to integrate my assembly into larger dynamic working assemblies in PDM. I sharpened my design for manufacturing (DFM) and project management skills, given a tight timeline and budget.
